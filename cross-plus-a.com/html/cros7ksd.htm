Killer Sudoku

In Killer Sudoku, sub-regions have specified sums, and no number can be repeated within a region. This variant is also called "Sums Sudoku", "Sums Number Place", "Samunamupure", "Kikagaku Nampure".

The formats of sudoku files:

  • KSD – Killer Sudoku (9 x 9);
  • KSD4 – Killer Sudoku (4 x 4);
  • KSD6 – Killer Sudoku (6 x 6);
  • KSD8 – Killer Sudoku (8 x 8);
  • KSD10 – Killer Sudoku (10 x 10);
  • KSD12 – Killer Sudoku (12 x 12).
