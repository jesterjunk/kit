Sudoku 4 x 4 ... 16 x 16

The program can solve puzzles of different grid sizes: 4 x 4 (the subgrids' size is 2 x 2), 6 x 6 (the subgrids' size is 3 x 2), 8 x 8 (the subgrids' size is 4 x 2), 10 x 10 (the subgrids' size is 5 x 2), 12 x 12 (the subgrids' size is 4 x 3), 15 x 15 (the subgrids' size is 5 x 3), 16 x 16 (the subgrids' size is 4 x 4).







The formats of sudoku files:

  • S4 - the puzzle with the 4 x 4 grid;
  • S6 - the puzzle with the 6 x 6 grid;
  • S8 - the puzzle with the 8 x 8 grid;
  • S10 - the puzzle with the 10 x 10 grid;
  • S12 - the puzzle with the 12 x 12 grid;
  • S15 - the puzzle with the 15 x 15 grid;
  • S16 - the puzzle with the 16 x 16 grid.
