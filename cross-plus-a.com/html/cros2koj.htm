Statistics

User can view statistics about text information in the program's database. To veiw statistics, choose the main menu item File|Statistics.

Available charts:

  • Word Length (amount of the same length words).
  • First Letters (amount of words starting with the same letter).
  • Last Letters (amount of words ending with the same letter).
  • Letters in Words (amount of words containing the same letter).
  • All Letters (amounts of letters in all words).
  • Amount of Vowels in Word (amount of words containing the same amount of vowel letters).
  • Amount of Consonants in Word (amount of words containing the same amount of conconant letters).
