Minesweeper Battleships

Minesweeper Battleships ("Battlemines") combines Battleships puzzle with Minesweeper game found on many computers. The aim is to locate the position of the fleet in the grid. The ships do not touch each other, not even diagonally. A cell with a number indicates how many ship pieces are adjacent to it. Ships are not allowed on the numbered cells.

Cross+A can solve puzzles from 5 x 5 to 14 x 14.
