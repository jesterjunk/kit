Word-combination Search

The program tries to find multiple word anagrams using the text letters.

For example: incredible = nice + bridle

The user can specify a precise amount of words in combinations or fulfil a Complex Search (search all word-combinations, which can be created). "Weights" of found words also can be shown.

Lengths of words can be limited by using special pattern (check box Use pattern for words length defining). For example: 7,6*,*4,9*14,*:

  • 7 – length of first word is 7 letters;
  • 6* – length of second word is 6 letters and more;
  • *4 – length of third word is 4 letters and less;
  • 9*14 – length of fourth word is from 9 to 14 letters;
  • * – fifth word can be at any length.
