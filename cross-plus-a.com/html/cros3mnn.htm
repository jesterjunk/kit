Cross+A

Cross+A is intended to help in solving of crosswords, word-based puzzles, logic puzzles (Sudoku, Nonogram, Kakuro, Hitori etc.). The program's database contains a lot of English words with descriptions. User can search words by known letters, patterns and descriptions as well as find different types of anagrams, add new words and edit the program's database. The nonogram editor allows to draw black-and-white and multicolored puzzles.

All additional information about Cross+A and its newest version can be found on our web-site and its mirror. If you have any questions, suggestions or comments about our program please direct them to our e-mail.
